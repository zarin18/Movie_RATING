 SHORT DESCRIPTION:
 Srabon Megher Din is a Bangladeshi drama film based on the Novel of Humayun Ahmed. 
This movie based on a triangle tragic love story set in a classical village of Bangladesh directed by the writer himself. 
This film was second film directed by Humayun Ahmed released in 1999 and won National Film Award.
