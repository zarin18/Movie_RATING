Short description:
"Jibon Theke Neya" is political satire on the dictatorship of Ayub Khan in then East Pakistan.This film portrays Bengali Language Movement of 1952 and tribute to the Martyrs of the movement. The story presents dominating image of a woman in a family consisting her husband, two brother and the servants.
